IDLab - Internet Technology and Data Science Lab

IDLab is a core research group of imec and its research activities are embedded in Ghent University and University of Antwerp.

Context-aware patient monitoring in hospitals through sensor streams demo:

Personalized eCare in hospitals allows to reduce cost and increase the patients satisfaction. In this demonstrator, we present a cascading reasoning platform that is able to combine the volatile sensor streams that monitor the patient with expressive reasoning to enable complex decision making, providing tailored care for each patient.
